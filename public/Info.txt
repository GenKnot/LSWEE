### 2.4.2 Technical Design | 技术设计

**English:**
The design integrates the most recent findings from Wuhan University of Technology's research on soldering materials. It aims to create a comprehensive soldering technology parameter database and employs a meticulously designed soldering quality control algorithm. The ultimate goal is to achieve intelligent control of laser welding equipment. In direct comparison with other products within the industry, our laser soldering quality surpasses the competition by a significant margin.

The laser soldering intelligent control system is a comprehensive solution that intricately establishes the heat flow coupling number, orchestrating the solder's flow and spreading dynamics under laser heating. This meticulous approach ensures a thorough understanding of the temperature field, flow field distribution, and shape changes during the solder spreading process under single-beam heating.

The versatility of the laser soldering intelligent control system stands as a testament to its adaptability and precision, ensuring seamless integration across diverse applications within the microelectronics and hardware manufacturing domains.

## 3. INDUSTRY ANALYSIS | 行业分析

### 3.1 Target Market and Use Cases | 目标市场和使用案例

**Use Cases in Microelectronics | 微电子领域的使用案例**

**English:**
Within microelectronics, the product finds application in precision domains, including FPC flexible circuit boards, camera modules, headsets, earphones, micro motors, micro-precision PCB, LCD, TFT, and other fields demanding high precision, density, and localized welding.

Traditional soldering methods face certain difficulties, especially in soldering flexible circuits for hearing aids. These circuits, designed for compactness and lightness, have a welding point width of only 1mm with 7 solder points per circuit. Our product mitigates heat absorption by components during the welding process through optimal parameter control, preventing heat-related burns in thin wires.

**中文：**
在微电子领域，该产品应用于精密领域，包括FPC柔性电路板、摄像头模块、耳机、耳塞、微型电机、微精密PCB、LCD、TFT以及其他需要高精度、高密度和局部焊接的领域。

传统焊接方法面临一定困难，特别是在助听器的柔性电路焊接方面。这些电路设计紧凑轻便，焊点宽度仅为1毫米，每个电路有7个焊点。我们的产品通过优化参数控制减轻了焊接过程中组件的热吸收，防止了细线的热相关烧伤。

---

**Use Cases in Connectors | 连接器领域的使用案例**

**English:**
In the realm of connector applications, the ongoing trend towards miniaturization and heightened performance in electronic products is reshaping the landscape. Taking the transformation of mobile phone connectors as an illustration, the primary focus is on the development of miniaturization and reliability. The pitch and height of connector pins are on a trajectory towards decreasing dimensions.

According to relevant data, the contact distance of the current standard connector, presently at 0.5mm, is expected to reduce to 0.3mm, eventually reaching an unprecedented 0.2mm. Traditional welding machines face limitations when dealing with connectors of 0.3mm and 0.2mm. Our product addresses this limitation by matching optimal welding parameters through an optimization algorithm.

**中文：**
在连接器应用领域，电子产品向小型化和高性能发展的持续趋势正在重塑格局。以移动电话连接器的转型为例，主要重点是小型化和可靠性的发展。连接器引脚的间距和高度正朝着减小尺寸的方向发展。

根据相关数据，当前标准连接器的接触距离目前为0.5毫米，预计将减少到0.3毫米，最终达到前所未有的0.2毫米。传统焊接机在处理0.3毫米和0.2毫米的连接器时面临限制。我们的产品通过优化算法匹配最佳焊接参数来解决这一限制。

---

There are established competitors in the industry:

3. **Enhanced Quality and Reliability**: Significant improvement in welding quality and reliability, tailored for microelectronics, connectors, and optical communication applications
4. **Adaptability to Emerging Industries**: Our product addresses the evolving needs of IoT, unmanned driving, AI, energy storage, photovoltaics, hydrogen energy, and semiconductors
